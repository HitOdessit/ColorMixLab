## How to read this credit

Every line of Kotlin, Swift, Gradle config, GitHub Actions YAML, and Markdown in this repository was generated by one of the two AI assistants listed above, under direct human prompting and review. The human author retained final say over architecture, design choices, and what shipped.

This is a portfolio project demonstrating AI-assisted development. The credits intentionally name the AI tools to be transparent about how the work was produced.
