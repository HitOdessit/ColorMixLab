## What Happens

1. **Before**: Normal intro screen
2. **Double-click**: Version number area
3. **During**: Full-screen celebration animation (~9 seconds)
   - Colorful circles bouncing
   - Circles merging together
   - Final burst with sparkles and confetti
   - Text: "🎉 Amazing! You completed all levels!"
4. **After**: Returns to intro screen automatically

## Tips for Testing

- **Speed**: Tap twice quickly (within 500ms)
- **Target**: Tap directly on the version text
- **Works**: In both portrait and landscape
- **Repeatable**: Can trigger multiple times
- **Safe**: Doesn't affect game progress or data

## Developer Notes

- Double-click threshold: 500 milliseconds
- Animation duration: ~8800 milliseconds
- No sound effects (visual only)
- Full-screen overlay
- Automatically dismisses
