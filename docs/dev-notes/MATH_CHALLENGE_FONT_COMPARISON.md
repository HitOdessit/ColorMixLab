## Design Benefits

### Balanced Layout вң…
- Question is readable without dominating the screen
- Answer buttons are the largest (most important for interaction)
- Better use of screen real estate

### User Experience вң…
- вң… Answer buttons are very large for:
  - Easy reading
  - Quick recognition in timed mode
  - Accurate tapping
  - Accessibility

- вң… Question is appropriately sized:
  - Clear and readable
  - Doesn't overwhelm the layout
  - Maintains visual balance

### Before This Optimization
When both were at 3x:
- вқҢ Question was unnecessarily large
- вқҢ Dominated too much screen space
- вқҢ Competed with answer buttons for attention

### After Optimization
- вң… Question: 1.5x - Perfect readability
- вң… Answer buttons: 3x - Maximum visibility
- вң… Clear visual hierarchy
- вң… Balanced screen layout

## Real-World Example

### Typical Math Challenge

**Question**: `2 Г— 5 = ?` (Medium-Large)

**Answer Grid** (LARGE):
```
      10        6        15
     HUGE     HUGE     HUGE

      12        8        18  
     HUGE     HUGE     HUGE

      14       11        13
     HUGE     HUGE     HUGE
```

The answer buttons draw your attention immediately, while the question is clearly visible but doesn't dominate.

## Device Scaling

The fonts still adapt based on:
- **Phone screens**: Uses lower end of range
- **Tablet screens**: Uses higher end of range
- **Portrait mode**: Optimized spacing
- **Landscape mode**: Adjusted layout

This ensures optimal readability and balance across all devices.
